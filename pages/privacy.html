Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

When you use Klaso, we collect: your name, email, phone number, location, profile photo, and any bio or job/listing content you provide. If you make a Pro payment, we collect the transaction reference you provide — we do not collect or store your card or bank account details, since payments are currently made by direct bank transfer.

2. How we use your data

We use your data to operate the platform: creating your profile, matching you with jobs/tutors/students, sending notifications, verifying accounts, and processing Pro upgrades. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Who can see your information

Your name, photo, bio, and public profile information are visible to other users, since Klaso is a marketplace built on trust. Your phone number is only shown publicly if you choose to enable that in your profile settings. Your email and password are never shown to other users.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a database provider with industry-standard security practices. Profile photos and uploaded resources are stored in secure cloud storage.

5. Your rights

You can edit or delete most of your profile information at any time from your account settings. To request full account deletion, contact our support email listed in the footer.

6. Cookies and tracking

Klaso uses essential technical storage (such as login sessions) to keep you signed in. We do not currently use third-party advertising trackers.

7. Children's privacy

Klaso is not intended for use by children under 13 without parental involvement. Student/Parent accounts are intended to be managed by an adult on behalf of a minor where applicable.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will note the "last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to our support email listed in the footer.
